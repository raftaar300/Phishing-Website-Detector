PhishingWebsiteDetection

Phishing is a cybercrime in which a the target is contacted by telephone, message or email by someone acting like a legitimate organisation or company to lure individuals into providing personal and bank account details to hack social media accounts or steal there money. There are number of users who purchase products online and make payment through various websites. There are multiple websites who ask user to provide there personal details and can use them for malacious purpose. This type of websites is known as phishing website. In order to detect and predict phishing website, we proposed a system that is based on using classification Data mining algorithm. We implemented classification algorithm and techniques to extract the phishing data sets criteria to classify their legitimacy. The phishing website can be detected based on some important characteristics like URL and Domain Identity , Web traffic and some other attributes
